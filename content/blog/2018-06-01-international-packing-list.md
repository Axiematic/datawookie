## Obvious

- passport
- cash or credit card
- waterproof
- smart kit (including jacket and tie if appropriate)

## Not So Obvious

- immersion heater
- pillow
- sponsor apparel

I get sponsored to attend conferences. It's always good to appear wearing the sponsor's apparel.
